Must-try Mexican Restaurants in Missouri

Whether you’re in it for authentic Mexican cuisine, or prefer something more on the side of Tex-Mex, Missouri has some of the best Mexican restaurants in the Midwest. Here’s some of the best Mexican restaurants to try the next time you’re traveling in Missouri.

Sunny's Cantina

This restaurant sits just far enough outside St. Louis to get away from the traffic, but close enough to stay connected to all the shops and attractions. Their Mexican-American menu includes twists on well-known classics, like asada fries and a rotating burrito of the month. Their diverse menu is sure to offer something for everyone!

Located at 6655 Manchester Ave., St. Louis, MO 63139

Call Sunny's Cantina at (314) 944-8226 or visit their website here.

Taco Buddha

Taco Buddha combines their “Tex Mex” roots with a modern twist in their small but delicious menu. Nearly every item on the menu is gluten free, and many can be made dairy free with simple substitutions. However- this isn’t just health food. The dishes are delicious, even for those with no food allergies.

Located at 7405 Pershing Ave, University City, MO 63130

Call Taco Buddha at (314) 502-9951 or visit their website here.

Abelardo’s Mexican Fresh

Abelardo’s serves authentic Mexican food fast. Their drive through ensures a fast experience, but the food is full of flavor. The convenience and speed of fast food meets the quality of authentic Mexican cuisine at Abelardo’s.

Located at 1416 Hathman Pl, Columbia, MO 65201

Call Abelardo's at (573) 443-0023 or visit their website here.

El Maguey

El Maguey offers a variety of authentic Mexican and Mexican-American dishes. Their extensive menu is sure to have options for all customers, regardless of their palette. Beyond the fantastic food, the environment is warm and welcoming, and servers are willing to answer questions or modify dishes to fit customers’ taste.

Located at 2201 N Belt Hwy, St. Joseph, MO 64506

Call El Maguey at (816) 232-5601 or visit their website here.

Los Arcos Mexican Food & Tequila Bar

Los Arcos prides themselves on providing affordable food that tastes great. They use fresh ingredients, and even make their guacamole to order. They also feature daily specials on various foods, and a free drink with every dish!

Located at 1212 E State Route 72, Rolla, MO 65401

Call Los Arcos at (573) 426-4388) or visit their website here.

Arzola’s Fajitas and Margaritas

Although Arzola’s offers several different dishes, nearly everyone visits for their signature fajitas. However, the fajitas are served on a normal plate, rather than a sizzling pan. Nearly every dish has a “fajita” component, whether it’s steak, chicken, shrimp, vegetables, a burrito or nachos. They pride themselves in providing friendly, efficient service that sticks to the core components of Tex-Mex cuisine.

Located at 2730 McNair Ave, St. Louis, MO 63118

Call Arzola's at (314) 226-9672 or visit their website here.

Taqueria Y Huaracheria Garcia

This hidden gem offers a variety of authentic Mexican food made to order. Whether you’re looking for tortas, burritos, huaraches, tostadas or just a simple taco, this is the place to go! They use fresh ingredients and offer a topping bar to dress your tacos to your personal preference!

Located at 3830 King Hill Ave, St. Joseph, MO 64504

Call Taqueria Y Huaracheria Garcia at (816) 273-5430 or visit their website here.
