Discovering Native Mussels in the Manitowish River

The North Lakeland Discovery Center's Woods and Water Staff embarked on a paddle adventure down the Manitowish River. Accompanied by Wisconsin's Department of Natural Resources (DNR) experts and enthusiastic locals, their mission was to search for native mussels and enhance identification skills. Guided by the expertise of their DNR companions, they gained insights into the habitats and significance of mussels within the river's ecosystem.

Rylee Alden, 2023
