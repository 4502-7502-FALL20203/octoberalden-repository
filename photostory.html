Discovering Native Mussels in the Manitowish River

The North Lakeland Discovery Center's (NLDC) Woods and Water Staff embarked on a paddle adventure down the Manitowish River. Accompanied by Wisconsin's Department of Natural Resources (DNR) experts and enthusiastic locals, their mission was to search for native mussels and gain identification skills. Guided by the expertise of the DNR and NLDC, participants gained insights into the habitats and significance of mussels within the river's ecosystem.

Rylee Alden, 2023
